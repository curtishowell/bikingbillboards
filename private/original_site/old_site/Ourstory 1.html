Our Story

Biking Billboards is a unique way to advertise your product or message in an environmentally friendly manner. We employ young people to ride bicycles pulling billboards in areas where thousands of people will see them. And these well dressed, responsible employees can stop and interact to answer questions, or hand out brochures, coupons, or any marketing materials.

The mere presence of our bicycles and billboards draws attention. People are curious. Check out our photos to see how visible we are!

And since advertising relies upon the number of people seeing the message, imagine 50,000+ people at a UW Husky Football game seeing your advertisement, in a "green" manner. Give us a call or email us and we can show you how we can help market your product!!
